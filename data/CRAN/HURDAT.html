HURDAT: Hurricane Re-Analysis Project

Scraped dataset of the Hurricane Research Division's Hurricane Re-Analysis Project known as HURDAT. Storm details are available for most known hurricanes and tropical storms for the Atlantic and northeastern Pacific ocean (northwestern hemisphere). See <http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/hrd/hurdat/Data_Storm.html> for more information.
