malariaAtlas: An R Interface to Open-Access Malaria Data, Hosted by the 'Malaria Atlas Project'

A suite of tools to allow you to download all publicly available parasite rate survey points, mosquito occurrence points and raster surfaces from the 'Malaria Atlas Project' <https://map.ox.ac.uk/> servers as well as utility functions for plotting the downloaded data.
