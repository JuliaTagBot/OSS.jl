spef: Semiparametric Estimating Functions

Functions for fitting semiparametric regression models for panel count survival data. An overview of the package can be found in Wang and Yan (2011) <doi:10.1016/j.cmpb.2010.10.005> and Chiou et al. (2018) <doi:10.1111/insr.12271>.
