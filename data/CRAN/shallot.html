shallot: Random Partition Distribution Indexed by Pairwise Information

Implementations are provided for the models described in the paper D. B. Dahl, R. Day, J. Tsai (2017) <doi:10.1080/01621459.2016.1165103>. The Ewens, Ewens-Pitman, Ewens attraction, Ewens-Pitman attraction, and ddCRP distributions are available for prior and posterior simulation. Posterior simulation is based on a user-supplied likelihood. Supporting functions for partition estimation and plotting are also provided.
