lg: Locally Gaussian Distributions: Estimation and Methods

An implementation of locally Gaussian distributions. It provides methods for implementing the locally Gaussian density estimator (LGDE) by Otneim and Tjøstheim (2017) <doi:10.1007/s11222-016-9706-6>, as well as the corresponding estimator for conditional density functions by Otneim and Tjøstheim (2018) <doi:10.1007/s11222-017-9732-z>.
