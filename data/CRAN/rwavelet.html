rwavelet: Wavelet Analysis

Perform wavelet analysis (orthogonal and translation invariant transforms) with applications to data compression or denoising. Most of the code is a port of 'MATLAB' Wavelab toolbox written by D. Donoho, A. Maleki and M. Shahram (<https://statweb.stanford.edu/~wavelab/>).
