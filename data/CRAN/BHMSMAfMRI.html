BHMSMAfMRI: Bayesian Hierarchical Multi-Subject Multiscale Analysis of Functional MRI Data

Performs Bayesian hierarchical multi-subject multiscale analysis of fMRI data as described in Sanyal & Ferreira (2012) <doi:10.1016/j.neuroimage.2012.08.041> using wavelet based prior that borrows strength across subjects and returns posterior smoothed versions of the fMRI data and samples from the posterior distribution.
