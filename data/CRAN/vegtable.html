vegtable: Handling Vegetation Data Sets

Import and handling data from vegetation-plot databases, especially data stored in 'Turboveg' (<https://www.synbiosys.alterra.nl/turboveg>). Also import/export routines for exchange of data with 'Juice' (<http://www.sci.muni.cz/botany/juice>) are implemented.
