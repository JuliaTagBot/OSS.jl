MRFA: Fitting and Predicting Large-Scale Nonlinear Regression Problems using Multi-Resolution Functional ANOVA (MRFA) Approach

Performs the MRFA approach proposed by Sung et al. (2017+) <arXiv:1709.07064> to fit and predict nonlinear regression problems, particularly for large-scale and high-dimensional problems. The application includes deterministic or stochastic computer experiments, spatial datasets, and so on.
