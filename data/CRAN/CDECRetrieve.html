CDECRetrieve: Retrieve Historical and Near Realtime Data from CDEC

CDEC maintains a set of web services at <http://cdec.water.ca.gov/queryTools.html>. In order to better interact and analyze the data this R packages allows users to quickly and easily retrieve data.
