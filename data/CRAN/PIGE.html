PIGE: Self Contained Gene Set Analysis for Gene- And Pathway-Environment Interaction Analysis

Extension of the 'ARTP' package (Yu, K., et al. (2009) <doi:10.1002/gepi.20422>) for gene- and pathway-environment interaction. A permutation and a parametric bootstrap approaches have been implemented for investigating gene- and pathway-environment interaction analysis (Truong, T., et al. (2014) <doi:10.1530/ERC-14-0121>).
