alfred: Downloading Time Series from ALFRED Database for Various Vintages

Provides direct access to the ALFRED (<https://alfred.stlouisfed.org>) and FRED (<https://fred.stlouisfed.org>) databases. Its functions return tidy data frames for different releases of the specified time series.
