OutliersO3: Draws Overview of Outliers (O3) Plots

Potential outliers are identified for all combinations of a dataset's variables. The available methods are HDoutliers() from the package 'HDoutliers', FastPCS() from the package 'FastPCS', mvBACON() from 'robustX', adjOutlyingness() from 'robustbase', DectectDeviatingCells() from 'cellWise', covMcd() from 'robustbase'.
