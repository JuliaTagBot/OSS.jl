zstdr: R Bindings to the 'Zstandard' Compression Library

Provides R bindings to the 'Zstandard' compression library. 'Zstandard' is a real-time compression algorithm, providing high compression ratios. It offers a very wide range of compression / speed trade-off, while being backed by a very fast decoder. See <http://facebook.github.io/zstd/> for more information.
