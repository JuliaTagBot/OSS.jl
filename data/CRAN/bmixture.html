bmixture: Bayesian Estimation for Finite Mixture of Distributions

Provides statistical tools for Bayesian estimation for finite mixture of distributions, mainly mixture of Gamma, Normal and t-distributions. The package is implemented the recent improvements in Bayesian literature for the finite mixture of distributions, including Mohammadi and et al. (2013) <doi:10.1007/s00180-012-0323-3> and Mohammadi and Salehi-Rad (2012) <doi:10.1080/03610918.2011.588358>.
