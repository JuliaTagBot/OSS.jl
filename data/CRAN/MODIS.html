MODIS: Acquisition and Processing of MODIS Products

Download and processing functionalities for the Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer (MODIS). The package provides automated access to the global online data archives LP DAAC (<https://lpdaac.usgs.gov/>), LAADS (<https://ladsweb.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov/>) and NSIDC (<https://nsidc.org/>) as well as processing capabilities such as file conversion, mosaicking, subsetting and time series filtering.
