PNADcIBGE: Downloading, Reading and Analysing PNADc Microdata

Provides tools for download, read, and analyse the PNADc household survey from Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics. The data must be downloaded from the official website <https://www.ibge.gov.br/>. Further analyses must be made using package 'survey'.
