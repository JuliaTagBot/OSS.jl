NlinTS: Non Linear Time Series Analysis

Models for time series forecasting and causality detection. The main functionalities of this package consist of a neural network Vector Auto-Regressive model, the classical causality test C.W.J.Granger (1980) <doi:10.1016/0165-1889(80)90069-X>, and a non-linear version of it based on feed-forward neural networks.
