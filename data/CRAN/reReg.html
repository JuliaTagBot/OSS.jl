reReg: Recurrent Event Regression

A collection of regression models for recurrent event process and failure time data. Available methods include these from Xu et al. (2017) <doi:10.1080/01621459.2016.1173557>, Lin et al. (2000) <doi:10.1111/1467-9868.00259>, Wang et al. (2001) <doi:10.1198/016214501753209031>, Ghosh and Lin (2003) <doi:10.1111/j.0006-341X.2003.00102.x>, and Huang and Wang (2004) <doi:10.1198/016214504000001033>.
