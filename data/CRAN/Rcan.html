Rcan: Cancer Registry Data Analysis and Visualisation

Tools for basic and advance cancer statistics and graphics. Calculates age-specific rate, age-standardized rate, estimated annual percentage rate with standards error. Creates graphics across variable and time, such as age-specific trends and period-cohort trends.
