smam: Statistical Modeling of Animal Movements

Animal movement models including moving-resting process with embedded Brownian motion according to Yan et al. (2014) <doi:10.1007/s10144-013-0428-8>, Pozdnyakov et al. (2017) <doi:10.1007/s11009-017-9547-6>, Brownian motion with measurement error according to Pozdnyakov et al. (2014) <doi:10.1890/13-0532.1>, and moving-resting-handling process with embedded Brownian motion, Pozdnyakov et al. (2018) <arXiv:1806.00849>.
