BayesESS: Determining Effective Sample Size

Determines effective sample size of a parametric prior distribution in Bayesian models. To learn more about Bayesian effective sample size, see: Morita, S., Thall, P. F., & Muller, P. (2008) <https://www.jstor.org/stable/25502095>.
