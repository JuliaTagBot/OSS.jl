cotrend: Consistant Cotrend Rank Selection

Implements cointegration/cotrending rank selection algorithm in Guo and Shintani (2013) "Consistant cotrending rank selection when both stochastic and nonlinear deterministic trends are present". The Econometrics Journal 16: 473-483 <doi:10.1111/j.1368-423X.2012.00392.x>. Numbered examples correspond to Feb 2011 preprint <http://www.fas.nus.edu.sg/ecs/events/seminar/seminar-papers/05Apr11.pdf>.
