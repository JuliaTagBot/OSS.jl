PELVIS: Probabilistic Sex Estimate using Logistic Regression, Based on VISual Traits of the Human Os Coxae

An R-Shiny application implementing two methods for sexing the human os coxae based on eleven trichotomic traits, following Bruzek (2002) <doi:10.1002/ajpa.10012>.
