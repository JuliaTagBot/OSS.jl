difNLR: DIF and DDF Detection by Non-Linear Regression Models

Detection of differential item functioning (DIF) among dichotomously scored items and differential distractor functioning (DDF) among unscored items with non-linear regression procedures based on generalized logistic regression models (Drabinova and Martinkova, 2017, <doi:10.1111/jedm.12158>).
