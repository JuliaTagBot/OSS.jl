spc4sts: Statistical Process Control for Stochastic Textured Surfaces

Provides statistical process control tools for stochastic textured surfaces. The current version supports the following tools: (1) monitors and diagnoses for local defects on stochastic textured surfaces, which was proposed by Bui and Apley (2018a) <doi:10.1080/00401706.2017.1302362>. (2) monitors for global changes in the nature of stochastic textured surfaces.
