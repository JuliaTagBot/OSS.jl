ROI.plugin.qpoases: 'qpOASES' Plugin for the 'R' Optimization Infrastructure

Enhances the 'R' Optimization Infrastructure ('ROI') package with the quadratic solver 'qpOASES'. More information about 'qpOASES' can be found at <https://projects.coin-or.org/qpOASES/>.
