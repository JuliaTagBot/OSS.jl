BayesNetBP: Bayesian Network Belief Propagation

Belief propagation methods in Bayesian Networks to propagate evidence through the network. The implementation of these methods are based on the article: Cowell, RG (2005). Local Propagation in Conditional Gaussian Bayesian Networks <http://www.jmlr.org/papers/volume6/cowell05a/>.
