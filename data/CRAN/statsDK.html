statsDK: A Wrapper for the API of Statistics Denmark

Makes it possible through simple functions and commands to get and organize official danish statistics within a wide range of subjects. Visit the <http://statbank.dk/> and <http://api.statbank.dk/console> for further exploration of Statistics Denmark data.
