echo.find: Finding Rhythms Using Extended Circadian Harmonic Oscillators (ECHO)

Provides a function (echo_find()) designed to find rhythms from data using extended harmonic oscillators. For more information, see H. De los Santos et al. (2017) <doi:10.1145/3107411.3107420> .
