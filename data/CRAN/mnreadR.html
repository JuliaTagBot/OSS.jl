mnreadR: MNREAD Parameters Estimation and Curve Plotting

Allows to analyze the reading data obtained with the MNREAD Acuity Chart, a continuous-text reading acuity chart for normal and low vision. Provides the necessary functions to plot the MNREAD curve and estimate automatically the four MNREAD parameters: Maximum Reading Speed, Critical Print Size, Reading Acuity and Reading Accessibility Index. Parameters can be estimated either with the standard method or with a nonlinear mixed-effects (NLME) modeling. See Calabrese et al. 2018 for more details <doi.org/10.1167/18.1.8>.
