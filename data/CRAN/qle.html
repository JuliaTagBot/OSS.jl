qle: Simulation-Based Quasi-Likelihood Estimation

A simulation-based quasi-likelihood method (Baaske, M., Ballani, F., v.d. Boogaart, K.G. (2014) <doi:10.5566/ias.v33.p107-119>) for parameter estimation of parametric statistical models for which closed-form representations of distributional characteristics are unavailable and can only be obtained by computationally intensive simulations of the model.
