MPN: Most Probable Number for Serial Dilutions

Calculates the Most Probable Number for serial dilutions to quantify the concentration (density) of microbes in a laboratory sample. Currently implements the confidence interval approach described in Jarvis (2010) <doi:10.1111/j.1365-2672.2010.04792.x>.
