softermax: Read Exported Data from 'SoftMax Pro'

Read microtiter plate data and templates exported from Molecular Devices 'SoftMax Pro' software <https://www.moleculardevices.com/systems/microplate-readers/softmax-pro-7-software>. Data exported by 'SoftMax Pro' version 5.4 and greater are supported.
