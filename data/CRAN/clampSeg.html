clampSeg: Idealisation of Patch Clamp Recordings

Allows for idealisation of patch clamp recordings by implementing the non-parametric JUmp Local dEconvolution Segmentation (JULES) filter, see F. Pein, I. Tecuapetla-Gómez, O. Schütte, C. Steinem, and A. Munk (2017) <arXiv:1706.03671>.
