ubci: Get Cryptocurrency Related Data by Upbit

Get 'UBCI' & Price from 'Upbit'. 'ubci' is the abbreviation of 'UpBit Crypto Index' and is the name of the index provided by 'upbit'. The 'upbit' is one of the cryptocurrency exchange in Korea. The 'ubci' package is a wrapper around the ticker information and 'ubci' API supplied by 'upbit'.
