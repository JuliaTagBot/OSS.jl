forestmangr: Functions for Forest Mensuration and Management

Set of functions for processing forest inventory data with methods such as simple random sampling, stratified random sampling and systematic sampling. There are also functions for yield and growth predictions and model fitting, linear and non linear grouped data fitting functions, and statistical tests.
