streambugs: Parametric Ordinary Differential Equations Model of Growth, Death, and Respiration of Macroinvertebrate and Algae Taxa

Numerically solve and plot solutions of a parametric ordinary differential equations model of growth, death, and respiration of macroinvertebrate and algae taxa dependent on pre-defined environmental factors. The model (version 1.0) is introduced in Schuwirth, N. and Reichert, P., (2013) <doi:10.1890/12-0591.1>. This package includes model extensions and the core functions introduced and used in Kattwinkel, M. et al. (2016) <doi:10.1021/acs.est.5b04068>, Mondy, C. P., and Schuwirth, N. (2017) <doi:10.1002/eap.1530>, and Paillex, A. et al. (2017) <doi:10.1111/fwb.12927>.
