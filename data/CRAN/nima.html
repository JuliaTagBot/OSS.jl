nima: Nima Hejazi's R Toolbox

Miscellaneous R functions developed over the course of statistical research and scientific computing. These include, for example, utilities that supplement existing idiosyncrasies of the R language, extend existing plotting functionality and aesthetics, provide alternative presentations of matrix decompositions, and extend access to command line tools and systems-level information.
