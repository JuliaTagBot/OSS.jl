UCSCXenaTools: Download and Explore Datasets from UCSC Xena Data Hubs

Download and explore datasets from UCSC Xena data hubs, which are a collection of UCSC-hosted public databases such as TCGA, ICGC, TARGET, GTEx, CCLE, and others. Databases are normalized so they can be combined, linked, filtered, explored and downloaded. This package is based on 'XenaR' package.
