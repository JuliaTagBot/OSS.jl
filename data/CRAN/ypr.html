ypr: Yield Per Recruit

An implementation of equilibrium-based yield per recruit methods. Yield per recruit methods can used to estimate the optimal yield for a fish population. The yield can be based on the number of fish caught (or harvested) or biomass caught for all fish or just large (trophy) individuals.
