Miso: Multi-Isotope Labeling for Metabolomics Analysis

An efficient approach for fishing out the dual (or multiple) isotope labeled analytes using dual labeling of metabolites for metabolome analysis (DLEMMA) approach, described in Liron (2018) <doi:10.1021/acs.analchem.8b01644>.
