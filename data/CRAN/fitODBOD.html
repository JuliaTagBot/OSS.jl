fitODBOD: Modeling Over Dispersed Binomial Outcome Data Using BMD and ABD

Contains Probability Mass Functions, Cumulative Mass Functions, Negative Log Likelihood value, parameter estimation and modeling data using Binomial Mixture Distributions (BMD) (Manoj et al (2013) <doi:10.5539/ijsp.v2n2p24>) and Alternate Binomial Distributions (ABD) (Paul (1985) <doi:10.1080/03610928508828990>).
