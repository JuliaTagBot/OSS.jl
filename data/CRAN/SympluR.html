SympluR: Analyze Healthcare Social Media Data from the 'Symplur' API

Analyze data from the 'Healthcare Social Graph' via access to the 'Symplur' API. The 'Healthcare Social Graph' contains billions of healthcare social media data points. Hundreds of published journal articles have leveraged data from the 'Healthcare Social Graph'. About the 'Symplur' API: <https://api.symplur.com/v1/docs/>. About 'Symplur' research: <https://www.symplur.com/healthcare-social-media-research/>. Credit to Professor Larry Chu, MD at Stanford University School of Medicine for the idea of the 'SympluR' package.
