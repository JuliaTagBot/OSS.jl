LPKsample: LP Nonparametric High Dimensional K-Sample Comparison

LP nonparametric high-dimensional K-sample comparison method that includes (i) confirmatory test, (ii) exploratory analysis, and (iii) options to output a data-driven LP-transformed matrix for classification. The primary reference is Mukhopadhyay, S. and Wang, K. (2018, Technical Report).
