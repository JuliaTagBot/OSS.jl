fdcov: Analysis of Covariance Operators

Provides a variety of tools for the analysis of covariance operators including k-sample tests for equality and classification and clustering methods found in the works of Cabassi et al (2017) <doi:10.1214/17-EJS1347>, Kashlak et al (2017) <arXiv:1604.06310>, Pigoli et al (2014) <doi:10.1093/biomet/asu008>, and Panaretos et al (2010) <doi:10.1198/jasa.2010.tm09239>.
