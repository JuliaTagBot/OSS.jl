SpatialPack: Tools for Assessment the Association Between Two Spatial Processes

Tools to assess the association between two spatial processes. Currently, four methodologies are implemented: A modified t-test to perform hypothesis testing about the independence between the processes, a suitable nonparametric correlation coefficient, the codispersion coefficient, and an F test for assessing the multiple correlation between one spatial process and several others. SpatialPack gives methods to complement methodologies that are available in geoR for one spatial process.
