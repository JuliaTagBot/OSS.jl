mlbgameday: Tools to Gather Data from Major League Baseball Advanced Media (MLBAM)

Multi-core processing of Gameday data from Major League Baseball Advanced Media <http://gd2.mlb.com/components/game/mlb/>. Additional tools to parallelize large data sets and write them to a database.
