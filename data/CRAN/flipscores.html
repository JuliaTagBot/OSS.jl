flipscores: Robust Testing in GLMs

Provides robust tests for testing in GLMs, by sign-flipping score contributions. The tests are often robust against overdispersion, heteroscedasticity and, in some cases, ignored nuisance variables. See Hemerik and Goeman (2017) <doi:10.1007/s11749-017-0571-1>.
