intccr: Semiparametric Competing Risks Regression under Interval Censoring

Semiparametric regression models on the cumulative incidence function with interval-censored competing risks data as described in Bakoyannis, Yu, & Yiannoutsos (2017) <doi:10.1002/sim.7350>. The main function fits the proportional subdistribution hazards model (Fine-Gray model), the proportional odds model, and other models that belong to the class of semiparametric generalized odds rate transformation models.
